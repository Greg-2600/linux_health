# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in our community is treated with respect and dignity.

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

- **Be respectful** of differing opinions, experiences, and backgrounds
- **Be constructive** in discussions and feedback
- **Focus on security** - report vulnerabilities responsibly (see SECURITY.md)
- **Be professional** in communication
- **Give credit** where credit is due
- **Ask questions** when you don't understand something

## Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Abusive, insulting, or derogatory language
- Threats or intimidation
- Spam or unwanted self-promotion
- Disclosure of others' private information
- Any form of violence or violent threats

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Contact the maintainers** privately via email
2. **Provide details** of the incident
3. **Be respectful** in your report

The community leadership team will:
- Review all reports confidentially
- Investigate thoroughly
- Take appropriate action
- Follow up with all involved parties

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussion forums
- Community channels
- Any community-organized events

## Enforcement

Community leaders will enforce this code of conduct fairly and consistently. Those who violate it may face:
- Warnings
- Temporary or permanent ban from community spaces
- Removal of contributions
